Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 8, 2025

This privacy policy describes how the mobile application "Tamil - Learn Writing & Speak" handles user information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how our app functions.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data from users. The app does not require users to sign in or share any personal information. We do not collect names, email addresses, locations, or any other identifiable data.

2. App Features

The "Tamil - Learn Writing & Speak" app is designed to help users learn the Tamil language in an easy and interactive way. The key features of the app include:

  • Clickable Words: Users can tap on Tamil words to hear pronunciation and see meanings.
  • Drawing Practice: Users can trace and practice writing Tamil letters and words using a built-in drawing area.
  • Save Feature: Users can save specific words or writing practice for future reference. This data is stored locally on the device only and is not shared or transmitted anywhere.

3. Advertising

This app displays advertisements to support our development efforts. However, we respect your privacy:

  • No Interest-Based Ads: We have turned off personalized or interest-based advertisements.
  • No User Tracking: We do not use any tracking technologies to monitor your behavior across apps or websites.
  • Ad Providers: Ads may be served by third-party providers (such as Google AdMob or Facebook Audience Network), but we have configured the app to disable personalized ad tracking.

4. Data Security

Since we do not collect or store any personal data, there is no risk of your information being leaked or shared. Any settings or data saved in the app remain only on your device.

5. Children’s Privacy

This app is suitable for all age groups, including children. Since we do not collect any personal data or use interest-based ads, the app is safe for use by children under the age of 13.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page. Continued use of the app after updates means you accept the changes.
